Discover Kerala's 10 beautiful villages, showcasing breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and serene rural charm.

25 Jan, 2025

Tisha Ghosh

Kumarakom: Nestled by Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom offers tranquil backwaters, houseboats, and a vibrant bird sanctuary.

Mararikulam: Mararikulam boasts pristine beaches, Ayurvedic massages, and delicious seafood for ultimate relaxation.

Aranmula:

Vagamon: Vagamon's rolling meadows and pine forests invite trekking, paragliding, and nature exploration.

Mattancherry: Mattancherry features a Jewish Synagogue, vibrant spice markets, and the historic Mattancherry Palace.

Meenangadi: Explore tea plantations, visit Edakkal Caves, and immerse in Wayanad's tribal culture.

Poovar: Experience backwater cruises, golden sand beaches, and visit Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary.

Athirappilly: Visit the stunning Athirappilly Falls, trek through forests, and explore nearby waterfalls.

Kuttanad: Explore Kuttanad's lush paddy fields, enjoy houseboat rides, and savor traditional Kerala cuisine.

Cherai: Swim at Cherai Beach, enjoy backwater boat rides, and watch playful dolphins.
